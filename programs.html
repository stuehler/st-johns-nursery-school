Programs

St. John’s Nursery School offers a specialized program tailored to the needs of three- and four-year old students. We offer a low student-to-teacher ratio for the most effective learning environment, ensuring that your child is given every opportunity to succeed. Additionally, children benefit from instruction delivered in small groups as well as one-on-one situations.

Various enrichment programs are also offered throughout the year. Specialists in music, movement, science and literacy frequently visit the school. Regular visits from our local librarian make story time extra special.

We are fortunate to have our own Literacy Specialist on staff full-time, who regularly works with students in small groups and writes a monthly literacy newsletter for parents which provides information on early and pre-reading skills as well as a recommended reading list.

Curriculum

We believe in an early childhood experience that focuses on the social, emotional, cognitive and physical (gross and fine motor) development of our students. Each day children have opportunities to work in large groups, small groups, one-on-one with a teacher or friend, and independently.

We follow the MA State Guidelines for Preschool Learning Experiences and the MA Curriculum Frameworks for Pre-K, for all subject areas. These subject areas include: Literacy, Science, Math, Social/Emotional Development, Health Education, Arts/Music and Technology.

We use a theme-based curriculum. We have seasonal themes as well as other themes which include, Author Studies, Space, Transportation and Dinosaurs. We change the areas of the classroom to correspond with the themes. We also read books and do other theme related activities.
